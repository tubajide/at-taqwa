WHY CHOOSE AT-TAQWA SCHOOL?

At-Taqwa Islamic Education school is important because it provides our Young Leaders with an environment in which they can learn and live Islam. The homogeneity of their culture and values create social and emotional stability which facilitates and accelerates their learning in general areas of education (math, science, language arts, etc.). At At-Taqwa Islamic Education School we believe in investing in the education, character building, and emotional and physical well-being of our Young Leaders.
